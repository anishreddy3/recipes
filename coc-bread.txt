Ingredients:
coconut flour : 1 cup
coconut milk  : 2 cups
eggs  :  3
Coconut oil  : 4 tsp
Baking powder  :  1 tsp
Salt  :  1tsp

Procedure:

Whisk eggs, coconut flour, coconut milk, salt, baking powder, coconut oil until they are properly mixed.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Then place the mixture on a baking pan for about thirty minutes or until
it gets golden brown.
